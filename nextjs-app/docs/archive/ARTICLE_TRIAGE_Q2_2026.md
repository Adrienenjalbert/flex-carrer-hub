- [ ] Ensure each H2 section starts with a 1-2 sentence direct answer

### Data Accuracy
- [ ] Add inline citations to every dollar amount and percentage claim
- [ ] Verify all source dates are current (2025-2026)
- [ ] Add source arrays to article data objects where missing
- [ ] Flag YMYL articles (tax, benefits, rights) for specialist review

### Brand Tone
- [ ] Check for banned phrases (BRAND.md list)
- [ ] Verify 70%+ you/your ratio
- [ ] Remove any AI slop patterns
- [ ] Check Grade 8 reading level

### SEO Value
- [ ] Verify keyword in title, first 100 words, and 2+ H2s
- [ ] Check title 50-60 chars, meta 145-155 chars
- [ ] Ensure 3+ internal links with descriptive anchors
